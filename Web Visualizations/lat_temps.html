Max Temperature vs. Latitude



Observations


A clear arch is visible on this graph with a max temperature close to 100 F at approximately 5 degrees. A steeper decline is visible in the Northern hemisphere than the incline in temperature in the Southern. This coinsides with a faster change in temperature over the Northern Hemisphere than in the Southern.
